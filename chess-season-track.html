TrifangX · Monthly season

Season track

One path per calendar month. Each step is a concrete in-game feat (openings, tactics, material, endgames)—not a generic grind. After you claim a step, the next one only counts what you earn from that point on in TrifangX, then claim the step on this page to add bonus points, cosmetics, and leaderboard flair to your account. (Claiming is what advances the track.) Stuck? You can spend shop points (the same balance you use in the TrifangX shop) to skip the in-game requirement for that step and still collect that step’s rewards (the underlying achievement stays unearned until you do it in TrifangX).

Reset this month’s season track?

This clears claimed steps for the month on your save, removes cosmetics and leaderboard flair from those claims, subtracts the matching season bonus career points, and resets your season earn baseline. Your global TrifangX achievements are not removed. Shop points spent on step buyouts are not refunded.

This cannot be undone.

Reset the June preview track?

This removes preview-granted cosmetics and leaderboard flair from the June admin preview track and clears preview claim progress on your save.
