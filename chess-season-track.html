TrifangX · Monthly season

Season track

One path per calendar month. Each step is a concrete in-game feat (openings, tactics, material, endgames)—not a generic grind. Meet the challenge in TrifangX, then claim the step on this page to add bonus points, cosmetics, and leaderboard flair to your account. (The achievement alone does not advance the track until you claim.) Stuck? You can pay a large chunk of career achievement points to skip the challenge check and still collect that step’s rewards (the underlying achievement stays unearned until you do it in TrifangX).

Reset this month’s season track?

This clears claimed steps for the month on your save, removes cosmetics and leaderboard flair from those claims, subtracts the matching season bonus career points, and resets your season earn baseline. Your global TrifangX achievements are not removed. Career points spent on step buyouts are not refunded.

This cannot be undone.
