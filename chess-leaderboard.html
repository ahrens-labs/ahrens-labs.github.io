TrifangX leaderboards

Rank everyone with an Ahrens Labs account by synced chess stats: points, wins, captures, and more.

Time range

All time uses full career totals from your cloud chess save. Last 7 days counts completed games in your synced game history whose save time is within the last seven days (when the leaderboard loads): wins, losses, draws, and per-move stats come from each game’s move list. Achievement points in that window come from each achievement’s unlock time and point value stored in your cloud save (new games after this update record both automatically). Very old games may drop off the capped history—favorite games you care about. Win % uses wins ÷ (wins + losses) on those games only. Points are career achievement points (not shop spend).
