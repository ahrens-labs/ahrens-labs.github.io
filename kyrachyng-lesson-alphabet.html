Kyrachyng • Lesson 1

Alphabet and Sounds

Learn the writing system first. This lesson organizes the alphabet into sound groups, then gives quick practice steps before moving into sentence lessons.

1. Learn the Core Letter Groups

Kyrachyng letters map to sound chunks. Start by learning groups instead of memorizing everything at once.

Stops and basic consonants

B b, C k, D d, G g, M m, N n, P p, R r, T t, V v, Z z

Special consonants

H th, J ch, S zh/sh, W ng

Vowels and diphthongs

A ae, I ai, O aa, Q oh, Y oi

2. Reading Practice

Read each symbol and say the mapped sound out loud. Then go to the full alphabet explorer to hear/see details and special notes.

  • Practice the consonant group twice (slow, then normal pace).
  • Practice vowels and diphthongs with example words from the translator.
  • Open the alphabet explorer and review every card once.
