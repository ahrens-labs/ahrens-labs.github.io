Grace Ahrens — graceahrens.com

graceahrens.com is the author home for Grace Ahrens, a fantasy writer who loves stories full of magic, adventure, and a few dragons. She has been writing since she was ten, with the goal of crafting tales that sweep readers away while touching their hearts and encouraging them in their own adventures.

The site includes an about page, a way to get in touch at grace@graceahrens.com, and a mailing list for occasional updates — release news, writing progress, and behind-the-scenes glimpses when there is something worth sharing (no spam).

Grace does not have a book published yet; the newsletter is the best way to hear when new work is ready.
